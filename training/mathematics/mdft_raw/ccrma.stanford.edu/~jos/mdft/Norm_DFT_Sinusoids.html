Norm of the DFT Sinusoids

For \(k=l\), we follow the previous derivation to the next-to-last step to get

\[\ip{s_k,s_k} = \sum_{n=0}^{N-1}e^{j2\pi (k-k) n /N} = N\]

which proves

\[\zbox{\left\|\,s_k\,\right\| = \sqrt{N}.}\]
