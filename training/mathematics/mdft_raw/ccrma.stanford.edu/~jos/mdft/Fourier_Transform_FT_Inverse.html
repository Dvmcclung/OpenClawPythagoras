Fourier Transform (FT) and Inverse

The Fourier transform of a signal \(x(t)\in\mathbb{C}\), \(t\in(-\infty,\infty)\), is defined as

\begin{equation}X(\omega) \isdef \int_{-\infty}^\infty x(t) e^{-j\omega t} dt, \end{equation}

and its inverse is given by

\begin{equation}x(t) = \frac{1}{2\pi}\int_{-\infty}^\infty X(\omega) e^{j\omega t} d\omega. \end{equation}
