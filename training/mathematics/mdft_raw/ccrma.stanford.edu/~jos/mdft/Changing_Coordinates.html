Changing Coordinates

What's more interesting is when we project a signal \(x\) onto a set of vectors other than the coordinate set. This can be viewed as a change of coordinates in \(\mathbb{C}^N\). In the case of the DFT, the new vectors will be chosen to be sampled complex sinusoids.
